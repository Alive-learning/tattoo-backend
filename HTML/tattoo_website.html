About OM TATTOO SHOP

12+ years of professional excellence

Founded in the vibrant streets of Mudaliarpet, Pondicherry, OM TATTOO SHOP emerged from a singular vision: to elevate tattoo artistry to a level of uncompromising professionalism. What began as an intimate studio has evolved into the region's premier destination for bespoke body art.

Our philosophy is rooted in the synthesis of creative rebellion and clinical precision. Every line drawn is a testament to our dedication to the craft and the trust our clients place in us. We maintain hospital-grade sterilization protocols, use single-use needles, and prioritize your safety above all else.
